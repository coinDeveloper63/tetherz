About TetherZ

TetherZ USD Bridged (USDT.Z) is a decentralized, bridged USD stable token deployed on Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20). Designed for speed, security, and transparency, USDT.Z aims to provide reliable on-chain USD-pegged value for DeFi, payments, and cross-chain transfers.

  • Network: Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)
  • Trust & Transparency: Contract verified on BscScan; supply and transfers are publicly auditable.
  • Use cases: Stable value transfers, on-chain settlements, liquidity, and DeFi integrations.
