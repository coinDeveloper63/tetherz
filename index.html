‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the backing mechanism of USDT.Z?

USDT.Z is a bridged stablecoin, meaning its value is backed 1:1 by reserves held on the native chain (where it originates, e.g., Ethereum USDT), and the tokens on BSC are minted only when corresponding assets are locked via a secure bridge protocol.

Why use the Binance Smart Chain for this stablecoin?

BSC offers significantly lower transaction fees (gas) and faster confirmation times compared to many other chains, making USDT.Z ideal for daily transactions, trading, and quick DeFi operations without incurring high costs.

Is the contract audited?

Yes. The TetherZ bridge and BEP-20 contract code have undergone rigorous audits by leading blockchain security firms. Summary reports are available on our GitHub page (linked in the footer).
